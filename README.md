# Department of English, SRM Valliammai Engineering College – Master Repository for Quests
Centralized Backup and Collaborative Management of 'Quests' for the Department of English Google Sites

## Vision
This repository embodies the **centralized archival and collaborative management** of all *Quests* hosted on the Department of English Google Sites. It ensures that every instructional module, embedded code, and multimedia asset is **securely preserved, systematically organized, and easily accessible** for faculty and student collaboration and continuous refinement.

## Purpose & Objectives
- **Preservation:** Maintain a comprehensive and secure backup of all Quests content.  
- **Collaboration:** Facilitate structured teamwork among faculty and contributors.  
- **Version Control:** Track changes over time, enabling restoration and accountability.  
- **Accessibility:** Provide a clear, organized framework for updating, editing, and expanding Quests.
